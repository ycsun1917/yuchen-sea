Seattle is teeming with artwork that we appreciate every day, artwork we forget is actually artwork because it’s so engrained in our routine, and artwork that we don’t even realize is there. We don’t always agree on whether the art is good—or even art—but there’s certainly something for everybody in our urban landscape.
